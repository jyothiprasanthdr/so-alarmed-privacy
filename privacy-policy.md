# Privacy Policy for So Alarmed

**Last updated: June 28, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how So Alarmed ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. So Alarmed is an alarm clock app for iPhone. It wakes you up and asks you to finish a short wake-up mission before the alarm will stop.

The App is built and run by Nandha Kumar Ilangovan ("the Developer"), who is the data controller responsible for your information under this Policy.

We designed So Alarmed to be privacy-first. We do not run ads. We do not use analytics or tracking tools. We do not collect crash reports. We do not sell or share your personal information for advertising. Most of your data never leaves your device. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. The short version

- The App works fully offline and without an account. Your alarms, settings, and wake-up streak are stored on your device.
- Signing in is optional. You only get an account if you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, and we use that account only to back up your alarms and streak so you can restore them on your other devices.
- We use Apple StoreKit for the optional paid subscription. We never see or store your card or payment details.
- The Shake and Walk wake-up missions read your phone's motion sensors in real time, on your device only. That motion data is never stored and never sent anywhere.
- We do not collect location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, health, or advertising data.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking.
- You can delete your account and all backed-up data at any time inside the App, under Settings then Delete account.

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect what the App needs to work. Everything is listed below.

### 2.1 Information kept only on your device

When you use the App without signing in, the following stays on your device. It is not sent to us or anyone else.

- Your alarms. For each alarm we store the time, an optional label you type, the chosen alarm sound, the chosen wake-up mission, the difficulty, the repeat days, and whether the alarm is on or off.
- Your wake-up streak, meaning the calendar dates on which you finished a mission and dismissed an alarm.
- App preferences and state, such as whether you have finished onboarding and your subscription status (see Section 2.4).

If you never sign in, none of this leaves your device.

### 2.2 Account information (only if you choose to sign in)

Signing in is optional and exists only to back up and sync your data. If you sign in with Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google, we receive and store the following through Google Firebase Authentication:

- A unique account identifier (user ID) for your account.
- Your name, if your sign-in provider shares it.
- Your email address. With Sign in with Apple you can choose Apple's private email relay, in which case we only ever receive a relay address and never your real email.
- Which sign-in provider you used, Apple or Google.

### 2.3 Backed-up data (only if you choose to sign in)

While you are signed in, the App backs up the following to your private cloud record so it can be restored on your other devices:

- Your alarms, with the same fields described in Section 2.1.
- Your wake-up streak dates.
- Your onboarding status.

This backup only happens while you are signed in. If you sign out or never sign in, this data is not uploaded.

### 2.4 Subscription information

So Alarmed offers an optional paid subscription that is handled entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. Apple tells us only your subscription status, for example whether you have an active subscription or trial, so the App can unlock paid features. We do not receive or store your name, card number, billing address, or any payment details. Apple handles all of that. Your subscription status is cached on your device only and is never sent to our cloud.

### 2.5 Motion sensor data (used live, never stored)

Two optional wake-up missions use your iPhone's motion sensors. The Shake mission reads the accelerometer to detect shaking. The Walk mission reads the pedometer, or step counter, to count steps.

This motion data is processed in real time on your device, used only to confirm you finished the mission, and then immediately discarded. It is never stored, never logged, and never sent anywhere. iOS asks your permission before these sensors are used, and you can turn that permission off at any time in the iOS Settings app. If motion access is not available, these missions fall back to a tap-based version.

### 2.6 Notifications

So Alarmed schedules alarms using Apple's AlarmKit on iOS 26 and later, and local notifications on earlier versions. These are created and delivered on your device by iOS. We do not run a push-notification server, and we do not send your data anywhere to deliver an alarm. If you start a free trial, the App may schedule a local reminder before the trial ends. That reminder contains no personal data.

### 2.7 What we do not collect

To be clear, So Alarmed does not collect any of the following:

- Precise or approximate location
- Contacts, calendar, photos, camera, or microphone data
- Health or fitness records
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA) or any tracking identifiers
- Analytics or usage statistics
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Anything used to track you across other apps or websites

## 3. How we use your information

We use the limited information above only to:

- Run the App's core features, which means scheduling and ringing your alarms and running wake-up missions.
- Keep your wake-up streak on your device.
- Back up and sync your alarms and streak across your devices, if you sign in.
- Sign you in and keep your account secure, if you sign in.
- Unlock paid features based on your Apple subscription status.
- Reply to you when you contact us for support or to use a privacy right.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or automated decisions, and we do not sell it.

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it for advertising. We share information only with the service providers we need to run the App:

## 5. Data storage, security, and retention

Where your data lives. Local data such as your alarms, streak, and preferences is stored on your device. If you sign in, your backed-up data and account information are stored in Google Firebase, specifically Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication.

Security. Data sent to and from Firebase is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and TLS, and encrypted at rest by Google. Your cloud record is private to your account. Even so, no method of sending data over the internet or storing it electronically is completely secure, so while we use reasonable measures to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

How long we keep it.

- On-device data stays on your device until you delete it in the App or delete the App.
- Cloud account and backup data is kept for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (see Section 6), this data is deleted.

## 6. Your choices and rights

You stay in control of your data:

- Use the App without an account. All core alarm features work without ever signing in, and your data stays on your device.
- Sign out. Signing out stops any further cloud backup.
- Delete your account and cloud data. In the App, open Settings and tap Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and all of your backed-up alarms and streak data from the cloud, in both Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Alarms already on your device stay there until you delete the App.
- Manage permissions. You can grant or remove Motion & Fitness and Notification permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app.
- Manage your subscription. You can manage or cancel your subscription in the App Store, under Settings, your name, then Subscriptions.

For any other request about your data, email us at inandha97@gmail.com (see Section 12).
